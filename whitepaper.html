1) Abstract

MANDU is a BEP-20 utility token developed by NOVA LAB (Tokyo, Japan) to bridge communication, payment, and blockchain into one unified ecosystem. Within the Mandu Messenger, users can send, gift, and shop using MANDU tokens, while UB Pay enables real-world payments through fiat currency and provides MANDU rewards. The goal is to seamlessly connect crypto with daily life ‚Äî making digital currency simple, inclusive, and borderless.

4) Ecosystem Structure

  • MANDU Token: The core asset powering the messenger, marketplace, and UB Pay reward conversion.
  • Mandu Messenger: Integrated wallet, P2P transfers, token gifting, and community commerce.
  • UB Pay: Merchant payment system that processes fiat transactions and converts part of merchant fees into MANDU rewards.
  • MANDU Guard Pool: 15% of collected fees are burned; another 15% are allocated to a stabilization reserve.

6) Technical Architecture

MANDU operates on a hybrid on/off-chain infrastructure. Inside the messenger, token balances move off-chain for instant UX; actual blockchain transfers occur when users withdraw to external wallets from NOVA LAB‚Äôs treasury. UB Pay serves as the fiat-payment layer synchronized with messenger accounts. Personal and transaction data are encrypted and isolated, compliant with Japan‚Äôs APPI and Korea‚Äôs PIPA.
