1) Introduction

MANDU is a utility token designed to connect communication, commerce, and crypto. Issued by NOVA LAB (Tokyo), MANDU powers an integrated ecosystem of Mandu Messenger (social + rewards) and UB Pay (mobile payments). Our objective is to make digital payments simple, inclusive, and borderless.

We envision a world where crypto is used naturally inside messaging ‚Äî sending value, gifting, and paying without friction. Inside Mandu Messenger, users settle in MANDU; offline, UB Pay lets them pay in fiat while earning points that convert back to MANDU. 4) Ecosystem

  • MANDU Token (Core): fee utility, loyalty, marketplace settlement.
  • Mandu Messenger: P2P transfer, gifting, digital items, in-app market.
  • UB Pay: offline fiat settlement; a share of merchant fees becomes user points ‚Üí convertible to MANDU.
  • Stability: 15% of fee revenue is burned; 15% goes to the MANDU Guard Pool for volatility mitigation.

5) Tokenomics

Flexible emission model: no fixed cap; 80,000,000 MANDU minted to date, with phased issuance aligned to growth. Fees arise from (1) messenger marketplace sales (seller fee), (2) on-chain withdrawals from in-app wallet, and (3) UB Pay merchant settlements. Of total fee revenue, 15% is burned and 15% goes to the Guard Pool (stability reserve).

6) Technical Architecture

Hybrid model: off-chain balances within Mandu Messenger for instant UX; on-chain transfers only upon user withdrawal. The company treasury (custody) dispatches tokens to user external wallets on BNB Smart Chain when requested. UB Pay interacts with the messenger (not directly with the chain) for fiat settlement; reward points sync back to convert into MANDU. Personal data and transaction data are logically separated and encrypted; audits will be staged before launch.
