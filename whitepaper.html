1) Abstract

MANDU is a utility token designed to connect communication, commerce, and crypto. Issued by NOVA LAB (Tokyo, Japan), MANDU powers an integrated ecosystem consisting of Mandu Messenger (social + rewards) and UB Pay (biometric payment). Our goal is to make digital payments simple, inclusive, and borderless.

2) Vision & Principles

  • Real-world utility: payments, rewards, and merchant programs.
  • Transparency by default: on-chain verifiability, reserve disclosure, and change logs.
  • Security: verified contract, staged audits, multi-sig treasuries, and LP lock.
  • Interoperability: API layer for Messenger/Pay integrations and future DAO.

3) Market & Problem

Payments remain fragmented between fiat, QR, and crypto. Retailers struggle to unify rewards, settlement, and cross-border acceptance. MANDU addresses this by delivering a token-centric flow from chat â†’ send â†’ pay with minimal friction and predictable fees.

4) Ecosystem Architecture

  • MANDU Token (Core): transfer of value, loyalty, and governance primitives.
  • Mandu Messenger (Q3 2026): encrypted chat, P2P MANDU transfer, merchant coupons.
  • UB Pay (Q4 2027): fingerprint checkout + MANDU settlement for physical stores.

Roadmap dates may shift for regulatory or security reasons.
