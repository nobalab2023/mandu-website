1. Scope

MANDU is a utility token on BNB Smart Chain intended for payments and rewards within NOVA LAB’s products. Integration to UB Pay (biometric payment) and Mandu Messenger is planned. This document describes purpose, token design, and a high-level roadmap.

2. Token Design

  • Standard: BEP-20
  • Total Supply: 80,000,000
  • Symbol: MANDU
  • Primary Utility: payments, in-app purchases, loyalty rewards, merchant programs

3. Utility & Usage

  • Payment and transfer functions via UB Pay (under development)
  • In-app balances, purchases and rewards in Mandu Messenger (under development)
  • Partner merchant programs and community incentives
No financial returns are promised. MANDU is intended for product utility only.

4. Roadmap (High-Level)

  • Q4 2025: Token info verification, website/whitepaper release, initial listings
  • Q1–Q2 2026: UB Pay pilot, Mandu Messenger wallet UI, merchant test
  • Q3–Q4 2026: Payment/reward features expansion, ecosystem partners

5. Governance & Compliance

The project maintains neutral, verifiable public information. Claims of performance or guaranteed returns are not made. Compliance requirements may evolve by jurisdiction and will be assessed as integrations progress.
