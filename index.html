About MANDU

MANDU is a BEP-20 utility token powering NOVA LAB’s ecosystem: UB Pay (biometric payment rails), Mandu Messenger (social + rewards), and partner merchants. Our goal is to make digital transactions as easy and rewarding as sharing dumplings — simple, inclusive, and borderless.
