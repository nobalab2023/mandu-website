Why MANDU

A token designed to live both online and offline.

MANDU is not just a meme. It is backed by an existing Korean food business in Tokyo and a long-term plan to link Web3 with everyday life – starting from one restaurant brand and expanding into a payment and rewards network.

01 · Real-world origin
Born from a Korean food franchise
NOVA LAB operates a Korean set-meal brand in Tokyo. MANDU will be used for in-store rewards, promotions, and later as a payment medium in partner shops across Japan and Korea.
02 · Mandu Messenger
Messenger-native rewards & identity
Mandu Messenger will introduce MANDU-based rewards, tipping, and badge systems so users earn from the conversations and communities they belong to – not just from trading.
03 · UB Pay
Fingerprint-based payment rail
UB Pay aims to combine fingerprint authentication with MANDU payments. The goal: a smooth, card-less checkout experience for restaurants, delivery, and cross-border usage between Japan and Korea.
Roadmap

From launch to everyday payment utility.

The roadmap focuses on building tangible usage: real businesses, real users, and a clear path from token launch to a functioning payment and messaging ecosystem.

Phase 1 · 2025
Foundation & Transparency
  • MANDU token deployment on BNB Smart Chain

Phase 2 · 2026
Mandu Messenger & community
  • Mandu Messenger beta launch
  • In-chat MANDU rewards & tipping

Phase 3 · 2027+
UB Pay & payment network
  • UB Pay pilot with fingerprint-based checkout

  • Progressive decentralization & governance
